Asurion Integrity First Game

When I designed this course - I used the brand guidelines provided by Asurion. They were looking for a starting point for their compliance training - and I happened to help design the "boardgame" for their anti-discrimination section of the training. I loved this project in particular because it already had some really nice illustrations, and brand guidelines that it made it easy to design accordingly. I didn't want to overwhelm the learner by putting a lot on the screen, so you can see that I compartmentalized each informational screen to not include a whole lot.
