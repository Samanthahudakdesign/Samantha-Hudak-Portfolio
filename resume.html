My passion lies within creating thoughtful and functional design. I thrive to combine visual, and interactive design to create meaningful experiences.

Education

Kent State University 17' — Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Communication Design

From the start of my college career - I knew I had wanted to be a designer. In highschool I was part of a CTE program called interactive media. We did everything from video shooting to photoshop - I even managed to give my classmates a lesson in illustrator! When I got to my junior year I started realizing that I had a passion for user experience and user interface. I took every project I could, and made it about UX and UI. Being part of the Glyphix team (VCD's student run design studio) opened my eyes to working with clients. I absolutely loved being able to see the client so happy at a logo design I had personally lead! During the last two years of undergrad, I aspired to be more well rounded, and worked a lot on my illustration skills (though it wasn't until after I graduated that I got really comfortable exploring that interest more) Now that I'm out of school I'm focusing on moving my career towards being a product designer, though thoughts of grad school are always in the back of my mind!
