Rolli App

Rolli is an app designed to bridge the gap between AllTrails and Strava. An app that is made specifically with skaters of all kind in mind. With what started out as a passion project, I've started implementing a design language, design system, user interface, and interactive prototyping as well as user testing on fellow skaters and friends.

Initial Thoughts

    There's not an app currently aimed at only skaters in the fitness app world.
    I have a whole group of test users for the Rolli app, why not utilize that?
    I'd love to make something inclusive and accessible for everyone in the skate community.

Research

    Asked members of my discord community what apps they use to track skates.
    Downloaded seperate fitness apps such as Strava, AllTrails, TrailLink
