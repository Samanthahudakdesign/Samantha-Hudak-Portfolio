Hey, thanks for stopping by — I'm glad to tell you a little more about myself!

I'm a graphic designer currently working in Ohio City — Cleveland, Ohio. I have a Bachelors of Fine Art in Visual Communication Design from Kent State University.

Professionally & personally my number one value I strive to put into everything I do — is empathy. Whether it be empathy for others hardships, situations, thoughts and feelings, or how a design could make a person feel & react.
